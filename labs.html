LABORATORY EXERCISES

LAB ACTIVITY #1

This lab activity involves creating a PHP-based patient information system for Philippine General Hospital. The form collects the patient's name, gender, age, number of days, and initial deposit. The system calculates the total amount based on gender-specific prices (₱150 for males, ₱85 for females) and applies age-based discounts. The net amount is the total after discount, and the balance is calculated by subtracting the initial deposit. A report is generated with all transaction details, including patient information, total amount, discount, net amount, and balance.

LAB ACTIVITY #2

This PHP-based application allows users to generate a customized multiplication table by inputting the number of rows and columns. The user simply enters their desired values, and the application dynamically creates and displays a multiplication table based on their input. It's a simple and interactive tool for visualizing and practicing multiplication.

LAB ACTIVITY #3

This application is about this Adamson University Online Registration system enables students to register for courses and calculate tuition fees. Students select their course (Information Technology, Civil Engineering, Accountancy, or Nursing), input the number of units, and the system computes the tuition fee based on the course's rate per unit. A lab fee of 2500 is added, with a 25% down payment for bills over 10,000, or 10% for lower amounts. The system also generates a receipt with the student's details, course, total bill, down payment, and remaining balance.
